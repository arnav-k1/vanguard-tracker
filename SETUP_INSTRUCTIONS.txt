# How to Deploy Your Dad's Vanguard Tracker
A step-by-step guide. Takes about 10 minutes. 100% free, no credit card needed.

---

## Step 1 — Get a FREE Finnhub API Key (no payment ever)

Finnhub is a stock data service with a generous free plan.

1. Go to https://finnhub.io
2. Click "Get free API key" (top right)
3. Sign up with your email — no credit card asked
4. After signing up, your API key is shown on the dashboard
5. It looks like: d1abc2def3gh4ijk5
6. Copy it — you'll need it in Step 3

Free plan: 60 requests/minute — way more than enough for 15-min checks.

---

## Step 2 — Upload the project to GitHub

1. Go to https://github.com and log in
2. Click "+" (top right) → "New repository"
3. Name it: vanguard-tracker
4. Leave it Public, click "Create repository"
5. Click "uploading an existing file"
6. Unzip the vanguard-tracker.zip on your computer first
7. Drag ALL files and folders from inside it into the GitHub upload area
8. Click "Commit changes"

---

## Step 3 — Deploy on Vercel (free hosting)

1. Go to https://vercel.com
2. Click "Sign Up" → "Continue with GitHub"
3. Click "Add New Project"
4. Find "vanguard-tracker" in the list, click "Import"
5. Before clicking Deploy, find "Environment Variables" and add:
      Name:   NEXT_PUBLIC_FINNHUB_API_KEY
      Value:  (paste your Finnhub key from Step 1)
6. Click "Deploy" — wait about 2 minutes
7. You get a URL like: https://vanguard-tracker-abc123.vercel.app

---

## Step 4 — Share with your dad!

Send him the Vercel URL. On his iPhone:
  Safari → Share button → "Add to Home Screen" → looks just like an app!

On Android:
  Chrome → three dots menu → "Add to Home Screen"

---

## How alerts work

- Toggle "Alerts" ON in the app
- Enter his email or phone number
- Set low/high price thresholds
- When price crosses a threshold, his email or text app opens
  with a pre-written alert message ready to send to himself
- The page needs to stay open in a browser tab to keep checking
